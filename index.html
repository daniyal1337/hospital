Services

Emergency Services 24*7

Our 24/7 services are here for you around the clock. We provide continuous care, support, and assistance whenever you need it, day or night. Your convenience and peace of mind are our top priorities..

ECG

Our ECG (Electrocardiogram) services offer quick and precise heart monitoring. Whether it's day or night, our ECG services are available 24/7 to provide timely cardiac assessments for your peace of mind and health

300 MA DIGITAL(DR) X-Ray Machine

Experience the future of radiology with our 300 MA Digital (DR) X-Ray Machine. This advanced technology allows for high-quality digital imaging, providing clear and detailed X-ray results. With cutting-edge features and capabilities, our X-ray machine is designed to enhance diagnostic accuracy and streamline the imaging process, ensuring efficient and precise radiological services for your healthcare needs.

Advance Physiotherapy Center

Welcome to our Advanced Physiotherapy Center, where your wellness and rehabilitation are our primary focus. Our expert physiotherapists provide cutting-edge treatments and personalized care to help you regain mobility, reduce pain, and enhance your overall well-being. With state-of-the-art facilities and a patient-centric approach, we are dedicated to optimizing your physical health and quality of life. Your well-being is our priority.

Pathology

At our Pathology Department, we specialize in accurate diagnostics and essential medical insights. Our skilled pathologists and advanced laboratory equipment ensure reliable results for a wide range of tests and screenings. We are committed to providing the vital information healthcare professionals need to make informed decisions and promote your well-being. Your health is our priority.

Modular OT Complex

Step into our state-of-the-art Modular OT Complex, where surgical precision and patient safety converge. We are dedicated to providing healthcare professionals with the ideal setting to deliver the highest standard of care while prioritizing patient well-being. Your health and safety are our top priorities.
